Bobnoxious

By John Doe for Citymedia.ca

When Bob Reid sings "Won't Go Quietly" you can tell one thing immediately -- it's true. Bobnoxious likes its rock loud and hard, like the Ramones updated for the 21st century, but with a bit more mischief. A live concert at the Aeolian Hall once was supposed to be recorded but the band's playing blew out the recording system.

Still, while the band has had a lot of radio airplay locally, it may still be one of Canada's best-kept secrets. They've even got a tribute band, which opened for them at their CD release party last summer.

The band is the brainchild of Bob Reid, former lead singer for rock band SFH and legendary metal band Razor.
