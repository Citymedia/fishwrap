Every daily newspaper in Canada

The only up-to-date website that lists all 155 daily newspapers in Canada -- including the 37 commuter/free dailies. Factoids: 123 are in English, 14 in French, 12 Chinese, three Korean and one each are Italian, Polish or Spanish. About 65 per cent are broadsheets. The most popular name is the News, followed by the Sun and the Star. Ontario is the province with the most dailies, followed by British Columbia, Alberta and Quebec. Toronto is the city with the most dailies, followed by Vancouver, Ottawa and then Montreal and Calgary.
