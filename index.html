Every daily newspaper in Canada

The only up-to-date website that lists all 116 daily newspapers in Canada -- including the 17 commuter/free dailies. Factoids: 87 are in English, 13 in French, 8 Chinese, four Korean, two Punjabi, one Italian and one Spanish. About 65 per cent are broadsheets. The most popular name is the News, followed by Metro and the Sun, then the Star. Ontario is the province with the most dailies, followed by British Columbia, Quebec and Alberta. Toronto is the city with the most dailies, followed by Vancouver, Montreal then Ottawa and Calgary.
